Top 3 Video Games

By: Nidia Rascon | February 17, 2025

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

One of the most immersive open-world games ever made. Explore the vast kingdom of Hyrule, solve puzzles, and embark on an epic adventure.

Red Dead Redemption 2

A stunning open-world Western game that tells a gripping story of outlaws, loyalty, and survival in a rapidly changing world.

Dark Souls III

A challenging yet rewarding action RPG that tests your skills, patience, and strategy in a beautifully dark fantasy world.
