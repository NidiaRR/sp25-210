Welcome to My Video Game Blog

By: Nidia Rascon | February 17, 2025

Why Video Games Matter

Video games are more than just entertainment. They help improve problem-solving skills, creativity, and teamwork. Whether you play alone or with friends, gaming can be a great way to relax and have fun.

Different Genres of Games

There are a lot of different genres of video games, from world discovery, RPG, zombies, fantasy to sci-fi games!

The Social Side of Gaming

Gaming isn’t just about playing, it’s about connecting. Whether through online multiplayer, co-op adventures, or esports, video games bring people together, building friendships and communities across the world.

Finding a Balance

While video games are great, it's important to balance playtime with other activities like studying, exercising, and spending time with family and friends.
